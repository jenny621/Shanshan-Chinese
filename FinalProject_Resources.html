Resources for Learning Mandarin Chinese

Photo by Aaron Greenwood on Unsplash

The resources listed on this page are from external sites that I am not affliated with. If you find any dead links or want to suggest resources not currently listed here, please contact me at jenny621880@gmail.com.

[Beginner] MITx: Chinese Language in Culture: Levels 1-3

This course series on edX assumes no prior experience with Chinese and focuses on getting the foundamentals like tones and behavioral culture right from the get go.

Learn More
[Intermediate] Happy Chinese

Happy Chinese is an educational TV show produced by CCTV for intermediate to advanced Chinese language learners in 2009. The show provides a highly entertaining way to learn daily life Chinese.

Learn More
[Advanced] Spring Grass (Chun Cao)

Originally a novel by Qiu Shanshan, Spring Grass was adapted for a TV show in 2008. "Perform Chun Cao: A Multimedia Advanced Chinese Course" is a textbook designed around the novel and show that can be used to develop oral and written narrative skills.

Learn More
[Beginner] Elementary Mandarin Chinese Textbook and Workbook

Written by Professor Cornelius C. Kubler, this textbook is packed with useful daily conversations, as well as detailed and insightful explanation on both the Chinese language and the Chinese culture. Continuing Mandarin Chinese is the next book in the series that brings you to the intermediate level of the language.
